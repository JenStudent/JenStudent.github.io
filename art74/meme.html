This is my one and only meme that I made. It ain't the best, but it will do for this page.

For my Meme Mashup, I was inspired by the failure (based on people reviews) of the movie from Sony Picture Animation, 'The Emoji'. So what I did was incorporate a meme of the movie and how people really feel about it, and have Sony Picture Animation reacted to it. I chose Pinocchio because my group as a whole decided to do something Disney related, plus that how I believe they felt when the movie failed horribly. To add to the shock factor, I also included a hand with a gun from a weird anime show, trying to show them debating if they should shoot themselves (end their company) for this movie or what.
