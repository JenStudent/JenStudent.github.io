As I was growing up, I was always a troublesome child. And thus, I made this short but hopefully interesting story that kind of represent my struggles as I was getting older and possibly shows that you are not the only one that has this problem. But nonetheless, I wanted to be something impactful. Something that you will always remember and used to overcome what problem you having in your life.
