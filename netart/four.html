And just like that, the smile on their face instantly disappeared into their skin, like it was never there to begin with. Now they became motionless once again.

You felt the sudden change from the figure was more horrifiying than when they were smiling. The absent of any form of emotion felt unsettling for you.

Then you heard it; their voice. It sounded strange; as though they have bever spoken in years. There was a small echo that came along with their words, similiar to someone talking in some corridors.

They were speaking now, and the words coming out of their mouth was unnerving. "We are the shadows that follow you everywhere." They said, as they begin to walk toward you.

Just as they said that, flashes of images started to pass before your eyes. Some disturbing, other worse. You saw silhouette of people walking, walking mindlessly, almost devoid of life.

"We are the one that you can never let go." They said, getting closer to you.

You then get flashes of a man in a happy mask, clutching his heart as though he have one.

Your heart was pounding furiously in your chest, unable to contain the fear from this situation. You yelled at the figure, obviously petrified. "Why are you doing this?!" You couldn't help but be afraid.
