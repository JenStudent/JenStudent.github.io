The silhouette of the person slowly turned their head to you. Something about their movement unsettled you. It felt wrong, unnatural.

Once their body was fully turned to you, you could make out their features, or lack of. There was nothing to distinguish their skin or clothes from their body. They were just all black. No color at all. None.

While they were all black, you were able to faintly see the outline of the their eyes. Their eyes, compare to their body, was a bit lighter in color. They were also closed as well, but felt like they were watching you.

You couldn't help but terrifyingly asked: "What are you?" It was a whisper, but it seems that the figure heard you.
